Rolex

Rolex is a Swiss watch brand and manufacturer based in Geneva, Switzerland.Founded in 1905 as Wilsdorf and Davis by German businessman Hans Wilsdorf and his eventual brother-in-law Alfred Davis in London, the company registered Rolex as the brand name of its watches in 1908 and became Rolex Watch Co. Ltd. in 1915.

Key Features:

  • Oyster Case: Introduced in 1926, the Oyster case was the first waterproof wristwatch case, setting a standard for durability and protection against the elements.
  • Perpetual Movement: Rolex's self-winding movement, the Perpetual, harnesses the energy of the wearer's wrist movements to power the watch, eliminating the need for manual winding.
  • Materials: Rolex utilizes high-quality materials like Oystersteel, a highly corrosion-resistant alloy, and Everose gold, a proprietary rose gold alloy, to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal.
  • Cerachrom Bezels: Many Rolex Professional models feature Cerachrom bezels, made of a highly scratch-resistant and color-stable ceramic, ensuring the bezel retains its appearance even under harsh conditions.
