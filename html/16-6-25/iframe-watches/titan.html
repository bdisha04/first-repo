Titan

Titan is a popular Indian brand known for its watches, jewelry, and eyewear. Titan watches, in particular, are recognized for their quality, diverse range of styles, and affordability. The brand, part of the larger Titan Company Limited, is known for its innovation and has played a significant role in shaping India's fashion and retail landscape.

Here's a more detailed look:

  • Brand: Titan is a well-known brand in India, recognized for its lifestyle products.
  • Watches: Titan produces a wide variety of watches, including men's and women's watches, smartwatches, and automatic watches.
  • History: Titan started in 1984 as Titan Watches Limited and has since grown into a major player in the Indian market, offering a range of fashion accessories.
  • Product Range: Beyond watches, Titan offers jewelry and eyewear, showcasing a diverse portfolio of fashion-forward products.
  • Impact: Titan is credited with transforming the Indian watch and jewelry industry and pioneering experiential retail.
