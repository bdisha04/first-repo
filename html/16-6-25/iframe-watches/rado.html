Rado

Rado is a globally recognized Swiss watch brand known for its innovative use of materials and design in watchmaking. They are particularly known for pioneering the use of high-tech ceramics in their watches, earning them the nickname "Master of Materials". Rado watches are recognized for their durability, scratch resistance, and unique aesthetic.

Key Features:

  • High-Tech Ceramics: Rado is a pioneer in using high-tech ceramics in watchmaking, known for its scratch resistance, durability, and lightweight feel. This material is often used for cases and bracelets, contributing to the watches' sleek and modern aesthetic.
  • Scratch Resistance: Beyond ceramic, Rado utilizes other scratch-resistant materials like tungsten carbide and sapphire crystal, ensuring that the watches maintain their pristine appearance over time.
  • Innovative Materials: Rado's commitment to materials science extends beyond ceramics, with the development of materials like plasma high-tech ceramic, which combines the qualities of ceramic with the shine of metal according to Rado.
