Fastrack

Fastrack is an Indian fashion accessory brand known for its trendy and affordable watches and other accessories, primarily targeting the urban youth. It started as a sub-brand of Titan Watches in 1998 and became an independent brand in 2005. Fastrack offers a wide range of watches, including analog, digital, and analog-digital options, along with accessories like eyewear, bags, belts, and wallets.

Connectivity & Smart Features:

  • Bluetooth Calling: Make and receive calls directly from your smartwatch.
  • Notifications: Receive notifications for calls, messages, and app alerts.
  • Music Control: Control music playback on your smartphone.
  • Customizable Watch Faces: Choose from a variety of watch faces to personalize your smartwatch.
