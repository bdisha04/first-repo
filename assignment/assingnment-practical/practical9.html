Hong Kong Disneyland

Hong Kong Disneyland, located on Lantau Island, is a magical world with eight themed lands and three hotels, offering a variety of attractions, entertainment, and dining experiences. It's easily accessible from Hong Kong's central areas and the airport. The park is celebrating its 20th anniversary with a year-long "Magical Party," featuring a new parade, stage show, and nighttime spectacular.

Location: Lantau Island, Hong Kong.

Themed Lands: Main Street, U.S.A., Fantasyland, Adventureland, Tomorrowland, Grizzly Gulch, Mystic Point, Toy Story Land, and World of Frozen.

Unique Attractions: Mystic Manor, Iron Man Experience, Big Grizzly Mountain Runaway Mine Cars.

20th Anniversary Celebration: "The Most Magical Party of All" with a new parade, castle stage show, and enhanced nighttime spectacular, starting June 28, 2025.

World of Frozen: The world's first and largest Frozen-themed land.

Transportation: Accessible by MTR (Tung Chung Line to Sunny Bay, then Disneyland Resort Line).

Special Experiences: Bibbidi Bobbidi Boutique, Duffy and Friends Playhouse, Tarzan's Treehouse, and Fairy Tale Forest.

Entertainment: Daily parades, nighttime spectaculars, live shows, and seasonal celebrations.
